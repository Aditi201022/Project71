school achievements

SIP ARITHMETIC GENIUS CONTEST (AGC)
secured the FIRST PLACE in the state round

SIP Arithmetic Genius Contest (AGC) is an All India Arithmetic competition for students of 2nd, 3rd, and 4th Grade where they are tested for basic addition/subtraction (for class 2 and 3) and multiplication (for class 4) skills. This year, AGC was conducted entirely in virtual mode where 3,25,000 students, 800 schools, 20 states participated in the preliminary round, held in November 2020. Akshitha of Grade 3B from Presidency School, Bangalore North secured the FIRST PLACE in the state round receiving a cash prize of Rs. 10,000. She also secured 4th place in the National Level finals held in February 2021. Congratulations Akshitha, we are very proud of you !!!

SOF INTERNATIONAL ENGLISH OLYMPIAD WINNINGS
secured 1st place

Presidency School, Bangalore North is happy to share the results of SOF International English Olympiad conducted in 2020-21 where our student Abhinav Muthanna Somayanda of Grade 7F has secured 1st place in his Grade, 11th in Zonals and 18th in the International Level. He has been awarded with gifts worth Rs.500/- a merit distinction and certificate of distinction. Keep it up Abhinav, we are proud of you !!!
